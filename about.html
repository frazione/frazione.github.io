Our mission is to create a community of researchers who can collaborate, build on each other's work, and advance the field as a whole.

There is a growing movement in science towards openness and transparency in order to improve the reproducibility of results. Frazione offers users the ability to test out the actual behavioral experiments that were used in human studies, providing access to materials and an easy-to-use interface. This makes it an invaluable resource for anyone looking to improve the reproducibility of their own research.

Our founding

Distill.pub undertook an ambitious mission to make the latest machine learning research more accessible. They built tools that let researchers share their work in an interactive, blog-style format. Frazione is a continuation of that mission. We aim to make the latest research in the field of Cognitive Science more convenient and hope to foster a community of learners who can interact with and learn from each other.
